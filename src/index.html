About

After completing Architecture, what next?

Are you striving to be a good architect? Do you aim to have a successful career in architecture?

Then vista is the venue you seek for!

VISTA is not just your typical college fest, it's a gateway for you to explore diverse dimensions that diverge from a single field: Architecture. Through interactive bubble talks with prominent figures who have leveraged their architectural foundation for various other fields, you will gain insights into alternative career paths. From field trips to activism, cinema screenings to promoting local art, VISTA promises you an immersive experience that will take you through broader horizons, widening the spectrum for the future. We are here to challenge your conventional viewpoints, provisioning you to envision a future brimming with possibilities. An exploration of dimensions, viewed from different perspectives, that's what we promise you. Join us as we redefine the boundaries of architecture and ignite inspiration for the visionary in you at VISTA!

Why stick to the same beats and lights? Join us to explore blueprints into the future. Something visionary awaits!
